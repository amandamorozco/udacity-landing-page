Small Steps, Big Change

    Get Started

    You’re tired of the same old mealtime routine--go to the fridge, rummage around between items of questionable freshness, too many leftover containers… well, you get the idea. You’re overwhelmed and hungry--and so is everyone else in your house. So you go for what’s easy and quick.

    Or, you want to do what you can to care for the planet, but are overwhelmed by the amount of products out there to “help you” (and are pretty sure most of those items are just creating more clutter and adding to the problem) and by “solutions” that require way too much thinking and planning that just don’t fit into your busy lifestyle.

    Not sure where to begin, or even if it’s worth the effort?

    Small Steps, Big Change is all about how to eat (and live) healthier, all while caring for the planet (and your wallet). Healthy change doesn’t have to be difficult, or expensive. It also doesn’t have to be complicated. But if you can take one small step today, and then tomorrow, and it becomes a part of your routine… well, the benefits can be exponential. So let’s get started--one small, simple step at a time. Each of the steps we’ll discuss here are designed to be simple and stand alone--no need for a complete overhaul of your home or routine. So you can adopt the ones that make sense for you, and get started. The key is to take those small steps so you can get headed towards big change.
